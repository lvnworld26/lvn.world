OUR STORY

LVN WORLD (Light Vision Night) was never born in a corporate room. It was sparked from a cold coffee cup at Cafe Wey, Tasikmalaya. Under the dim city lights and midnight breeze, a 17-year-old visionary decided to stop dreaming and start a movement.

While the world sleeps, the midnight wanderers awake. For us, the night is a blank canvas where raw urban aesthetics and visual distortions take over. LVN is a dedicated space for those who stay up, create, and redefine the rules in the dark.

From Tasikmalaya to the global scene, we don't just manufacture garments. Every heavyweight Cotton Combed 20s fabric and precise plastisol ink we select is a testament to youth ambition—proving that vision has no age limits.
